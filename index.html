undercode

undercode is an Open source project and a Non-profit organization looking to provide free and accessible resources to developers and web designers alike. The platform aims to bring everything together in one community.

Our idea

As we start to learn to code and not often have engouh money to purchase paid courses, we mostly like to go for the free available sources, which exist a lot, but are way too much spread all over the internet.
Our idea is to turn that around and create a place where all the new & the more advanced developers or designers can come around and get all that information in one place!

The undercode project is an open-sourced one, open to all developers who wish to contribute and help grow the technology ecosystem. With that said, we are an Open Source community; creating and giving.
